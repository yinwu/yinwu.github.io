多生成树协议
=======================


tech stack selection
--------------------

**Function**

The overall technical requirements of most projects must be met	

**Mainstream**

Must be the winner of new technologies in recent years	

**Open source**

Supports well open source license	

**Standard**

Has Implemented the industry’s main standards and protocols	

**Support**

Well supports from open community and well-documented	

**Future**

 Will continue to be the mainstream in next years	

**Enterprise**

Easy to use in big enterprises, easy to uniform in global teams	

**Cost**

Cheap or no cost	

**Performance**

Select the winner according to benchmark evaluations	

**Practice**

Select which has better and more practices in industry, and has general consensus among all teams 	

**Agile**

Must be appropriate for today’s fast and agile development process
